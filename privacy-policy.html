GentleTrack - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data Collection

GentleTrack does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data stays on your device.

Health and Wellness Data

GentleTrack collects and stores the following health and wellness data locally on your device only:

  • Habit tracking data: Names of habits you create, daily completion logs, and numeric/time values you record (e.g., glasses of water, minutes of exercise)
  • Mood data: Daily mood ratings (1-5 scale) and optional mood notes you enter
  • Streak and consistency data: Calculated from your habit logs to show progress insights

This data is used solely to provide you with habit tracking, calendar views, and insights within the app. It is never transmitted to any server, shared with third parties, or used for advertising or analytics purposes.

Other Data Stored Locally

  • App preferences (theme, reminder settings, week start day)
  • Purchase status (whether premium has been unlocked)
  • Notification schedules (stored on-device only)

Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device for as long as you use the app. You have full control over your data:

  • Delete individual data: You can delete individual habit logs, habits, and mood entries at any time within the app
  • Reset all data: Use the "Reset All Data" option in Settings to permanently delete all app data
  • Uninstall the app: Deleting the app from your device permanently removes all stored data
  • Export before deletion: You can export your data as CSV or JSON backup before deleting it

GentleTrack does not retain any data after the app is uninstalled. There are no server-side backups or copies of your data.

In-App Purchases

GentleTrack offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock unlimited habits. Purchase transactions are processed by Google Play (Android) or the App Store (iOS). We use RevenueCat to manage purchase status. RevenueCat may receive an anonymous app user ID and purchase receipt — no personal information is shared. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details.

No Accounts

No login, signup, or personal information is required to use GentleTrack.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not collect usage data or device identifiers.

Notifications

GentleTrack uses local notifications for daily reminders. These are scheduled on your device and do not involve any external server.

Data Export

When you export your data (CSV or JSON backup), the file is generated locally on your device. Sharing is handled by your device's built-in sharing mechanism.

Children's Privacy

GentleTrack is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
